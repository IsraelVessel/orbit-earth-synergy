Space Data for Earth Solutions

Leveraging NASA's Earth observation data, space biology research, and commercial space insights to address global challenges and advance exploration.

- SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities - The project focuses on urban health and sustainability, utilizing NASA Earth observation data to monitor and visualize environmental factors affecting cities.
- SDG 3: Good Health and Well-being - By addressing air quality and urban health, the project contributes to improving the well-being of citizens, particularly in Nigerian cities like Lagos, Abuja, Ibadan, and Kano.
- SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure - The development of a modular, scalable application leveraging NASA data and space biology research promotes innovation and sustainable infrastructure.
- SDG 13: Climate Action - Although not the primary focus, the project's use of NASA data for environmental monitoring and simulation can contribute to climate action by informing decision-makers and promoting sustainable practices.
- SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals - The project's collaboration with NASA and utilization of their data highlights the importance of global partnerships in achieving sustainable development goals.
